99 Balls: A Simple Yet Addictive Arcade Game

99 Balls is a straightforward yet highly entertaining 2D arcade game that blends the simplicity of bouncing balls with challenging mechanics inspired by classic games like Breakout and Pinball. The objective is simple: bounce your balls off spheres descending from the top of the screen to break them. As the game progresses, the challenge increases, requiring strategic thinking and quick reflexes.

How to Play 99 Balls

In 99 Balls, you use your mouse to control the trajectory of the balls. By dragging the cursor, you can aim and release the left mouse button to send the balls bouncing. Your goal is to hit the colored spheres with the balls to break them and clear space on the screen. Each sphere has a number, indicating how many bounces it needs to break. If any sphere reaches the bottom, the game ends.
